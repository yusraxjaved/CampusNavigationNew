Admission Process

Follow our simple step-by-step process to begin your educational journey at HITEC University

1. Submit Application

Complete the online application form and submit required documents

2. Entrance Test

Take the HITEC entrance examination or submit SAT/ACT scores

3. Interview

Attend an interview with the department faculty committee

4. Decision

Receive admission decision and complete enrollment process

Admission Requirements

Ensure you meet our admission criteria before applying

Academic Requirements

  • Minimum 60% marks in FSc/A-Levels or equivalent
  • SAT I/II scores (for international students)
  • English language proficiency

Required Documents

  • Academic transcripts
  • Valid CNIC/Passport copy
  • Passport size photographs
